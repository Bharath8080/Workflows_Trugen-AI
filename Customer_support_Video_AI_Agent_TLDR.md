# Create a customer support voice & video agent with GPT-4.1 mini and Google Workspace booking

This n8n template creates a high-performance AI operations agent designed for service platforms. Optimized for **Trugen AI**'s high-performance, low-latency voice and video interfaces, it handles user requests with intelligent reasoning and multi-tool integration, ensuring a professional and seamless support experience.

- **Service-based businesses** that want to provide 24/7 automated support and scheduling.
- **AI Developers** building conversational video agents using the Trugen AI platform.
- **Support teams** looking to automate repetitive FAQs and booking confirmations.
- **Entrepreneurs** aiming to scale customer engagement with multimodal AI.

## üí° What problem is this workflow solving?

Traditional support systems often struggle with:

- **Repeated Inquiries**: Human agents spending hours answering the same questions.
- **Manual Scheduling**: Friction in booking meetings and sending follow-up emails.
- **Fragmented Data**: Difficulty in syncing real-time voice interactions with business tools.

This workflow solves these by using **OpenAI GPT-4.1 mini** reasoning to intelligently handle queries, retrieve knowledge from **Google Sheets**, and manage **Google Calendar** bookings automatically.

## ‚öôÔ∏è What this workflow does

- **Captures Input**: Receives live voice/video transcriptions via a secure Webhook from Trugen AI.
- **Interprets Intent**: Uses an AI Agent with specialized reasoning to decide between answering questions or scheduling appointments.
- **Retrieves Knowledge**: Searches your Google Sheets database for service details, pricing, or platform info.
- **Schedules Meetings**: Checks real-time availability and creates events in Google Calendar (with IST timezone support).
- **Sends Confirmations**: Automatically triggers a Gmail notification once a booking is successfully completed.
- **Low-Latency Interaction**: Powered by **Trugen AI** for near-instant responses in voice and video conversations.

## üß∞ Setup

- **OpenAI**: Connect your API key (optimized for GPT-4.1 mini).
- **Google Workspace**: Enable and connect Google Sheets, Calendar, and Gmail.
- **Database**: Update the `documentId` in the Google Sheets node to point to your FAQ/knowledgebase.
- **Webhook**: Replace the Webhook URL in your Trugen AI agent settings with the one generated by this workflow.
- **Prompting**: Follow the detailed system prompt and platform setup instructions in the [Trugen AI Readme](trugeni-ai-readme.md).

- **Knowledgehouse**: Simply update your Google Sheet rows to add new services or FAQ answers.
